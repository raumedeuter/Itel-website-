Product Philosophy

Our products are designed around three core principles: save money, maximize safety, and ensure reliable performance in real-world conditions.

Save Money

  • One system compatible with both on & off-grid scenarios
  • Modular, scalable and configurable design
  • AI-optimized battery life for lower total cost

Safety

  • Multi-layer battery safety protections
  • Innovative DC ground-fault protection
  • Real-time risk prevention with data-driven monitoring

Reliable

  • Intelligent BMS with comprehensive protection
  • High discharge capability and wide-temp operation
