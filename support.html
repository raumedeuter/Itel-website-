Frequently Asked Questions

What is the warranty period for my product?

▼
Warranty periods vary by product series: • IP21 Series: 1 Year Replacement • IP31 Series: 2 Years Replacement • IP54 Series: 3 Years Replacement • IP66 Series: 5 Years Replacement • Lithium Battery: 5 Years

How do I register my product for warranty?

▼
Product warranty is automatically valid from the first installation date. Registration helps us provide better support. Contact our support team with your serial number and installation date to register your product.

What are the storage requirements for my battery?

▼
• Storage SOC: 20%~50% • Storage Temperature: -25℃ to 35℃ (for less than 6 months) • Storage Humidity: 5% RH to 95% RH (no condensation) • Charge and discharge the battery once every 6 months • Keep away from direct sunlight, rain, and flammable substances

What operating temperature range is recommended?

▼
• Operating Temperature Range: -20℃ to 55℃ • Recommended Operating Temperature: 10℃ to 30℃ Operating outside these ranges may affect battery life and performance. Please ensure proper installation location with adequate ventilation.

Does Sunce-iTel Energy update products remotely?

▼
Yes. Sunce-iTel Energy periodically updates devices through remote firmware upgrades to improve functionality and performance. These updates may briefly interrupt operation. By connecting to the internet, you agree to automatic updates. If your device is offline for a month, we'll notify you to reconnect.

What does the warranty NOT cover?

▼
The warranty does not cover: • Damage from improper installation or use • Unauthorized disassembly or repairs • Force majeure (storms, lightning, floods, etc.) • Cosmetic wear and tear • Transportation damage • Water ingress or voltage fluctuations • Installation in coastal areas within 500m of coastline • Removed or altered warranty stamps and serial numbers
